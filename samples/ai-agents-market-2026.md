# AI Agents Market Analysis 2026

**Research Brief | February 2026**

## Executive Summary

The AI agents market is experiencing explosive growth, projected to reach $47B by 2028. Key trends include the rise of autonomous coding agents, personal AI assistants, and enterprise automation tools. The market is shifting from experimental to production-ready, with major players (Anthropic, OpenAI, Google) racing to capture market share.

1. **Agentic Workflows:** Agents that chain multiple tasks autonomously
2. **MCP (Model Context Protocol):** Standardized tool interfaces gaining adoption
3. **Local-First Agents:** Privacy-focused on-device AI gaining traction
4. **Vertical Specialists:** Domain-specific agents (legal, medical, finance)
